Philosophy.

I think the best – businesses, people, ideas – are led by goals and virtues, not by empty showmanship. Good design should help in the same way: to clarify a message, rather than to distort or parade it. I work with brands whose values are best stated clearly and honestly. Simplicity not for its own sake, but for the sake of comfortably and honestly sharing missions and values.
