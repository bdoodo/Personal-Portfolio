Designing and building web apps that mean something. I'm primarily focused on front-end development and working with serverless technologies, but I'm also passionate about development's deep connection to ux and design.

Philosophy.

Designs influence cultures and the way we live with and talk to one another. That's powerful, and to accommodate this, good design should be purposeful: Strong when needed, hidden when not, yet always backed with intention.

As something functional, I see ux design as intertwined with development, in the way that architecture is intertwined with the engineering and construction that follows. This was my own entry into web development: To see it as a tool of digital design, necessary yet not less interesting.

Mainly, I'm passionate about the future of the web, especially as it'll evolve on the Jamstack.
