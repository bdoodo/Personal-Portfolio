Zentella

Organic, playful concept
for a beauty brand.

Zentella was an emerging beauty brand focused on ethics and sustainability as much as it was on quality. In a sea of mass-commercial products, I wanted to create branding elements to give the website its own personality while staying grassroots.

Role

UI/UX
Illustrations

Artwork

To build on the existing branding material and the founder's own personality, I chose the theme words quiet, feminine, and playful. Hand-drawn illustrations provide a part of this feel: Free, loose forms keep them personal rather than mechanical.

This GIF is meant to symbolize decomposition. Because Zentella's products have compostable packaging, it builds on the theme of sustainability, a core idea behind the brand.

Typography

Typography is one of my main considerations; in the space of the page designated for "lorem ipsum …," it carries the voice of a brand, and styles the page itself. For a feminine tone, I started my search with female typographers and narrowed it with other criteria listed in the caption below.

I believe typographic details matter - the balancing of light and heavier weights, emphasis with italics or drop-caps, paragraph indentations, and added visual interest to break up text. These small details lead to a lasting professional image.
